Sangcon Gravity Electric Salt and Pepper Grinder Set

Transform your kitchen routine with the Sangcon Gravity Electric Salt and Pepper Grinder Set. This smart and modern tool is designed to bring convenience and precision to every meal. With its gravity sensing technology, you can simply flip the grinder over and let it do the work automatically, eliminating the need for manual pressing or buttons.

Whether you're seasoning your favorite dishes with a fine powder or a bold, chunky spice, the adjustable predictor ring lets you control the grind consistency with ease. The set includes a large capacity of 184g/6.5oz for sea salt and 100g/3.5oz for white pepper, keeping your kitchen stocked without frequent refills. Plus, the built-in blue LED light ensures clear visibility even in dim lighting, making it perfect for both morning preparations and evening cooking.

Featuring a sleek, food-grade acrylic bottle, this electric salt and pepper grinder set combines functionality with style. It's an ideal choice for home cooks, food lovers, and those who enjoy a smooth, mess-free way to season. Elevate your kitchen experience and make every dish a flavorful delight.
